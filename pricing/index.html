Frequently asked questions

Your legacy. Their memories.

1. How far in advance do you recommend booking the wedding photography reportage?

The best way to ensure that I will be available on your wedding day is to book at least 6 months in advance. This way I will mark your day in my calendar and you will no longer have to suffer because of my availability.

2. Is it necessary to pay part of the cost at the time of reservation?

In order to complete the reservation of your day, it is necessary to pay 25% of the total cost of the report. Only in this way is the reservation formalized. The rest of the payment is made the day after the wedding or during the week following the wedding.

3. How long does it take to deliver the full report?

Digital images are delivered within two to three weeks. They are delivered in the highest quality through WeTransfer or similar. The printed album is made once the digital images have been delivered and is delivered 20 days later.

4. What if it rains or the weather is bad on the wedding day?

Both myself and all my photography equipment are 100% ready for bad weather. If inclement weather allows the wedding to take place, you don't have to worry that the quality of my photos will be affected.

5. How many photographs do you deliver?

I like to adopt the concept of “free photos”. The cost of my services covers my talent, time, physical material and editing technique. I guarantee a delivery of between 700 and 1,200 images depending on the length of the wedding.

6. Will we have to pose during the wedding day?

A wedding shouldn't turn into a photo shoot and I'm not really into posing photography. I try to intervene as little as possible and go completely unnoticed. Only if you feel like it, once the nerves before the ceremony have passed, I like to do a more intimate session with the couple with very simple directions.

7. Do you work by the hour? When do your wedding day services end?

I do not work by the hour, as I consider each wedding to be unique and special. If you choose the full reporting option, you will have my services throughout the day. The report is considered complete when you can no longer dance.

8. How far can you travel?

I can travel to anywhere in the world. So don't hesitate to submit any proposal to me. I will go wherever your love story takes you.

9. Why is the engagement session so important?

This session is the best way to get to know each other with the camera in the middle. It lets you familiarize yourself with my working style, lose the fear of being photographed, and get intimate and relaxed images before the wedding day.

10. Why are your albums so special?

The albums are fully customizable and each print is unique. Once I have given you all the edited digital wedding images, I will ask you to select 30-50 photographs. Then I design the layout, show you the mockup, and send it to the printer once the result is right.

About me

Hi, I’m Xavi!

My interest in photography was born six years ago when I started the Journalism degree. And it was during my internship at Vanguardia that what had started as a simple hobby turned into a real passion.

I dedicated my first years to local photojournalism. I was passionate about being able to meet and tell stories through my camera. But I immediately understood that I needed to go further. I was looking for stories where happiness and love were the main protagonists. And I found this reality in weddings.

Since then I have dedicated myself fully to wedding photography. For me it is an honor and a real satisfaction to be able to photograph these very special moments. Every wedding is different and a new story to tell.

Natural images, honest stories

So I wish with all my heart to know your story and start working together.

In my reports I like to portray the moments of fun, the tears and those elements that make your day unique and special. Thanks to my journalistic roots I like to capture emotions and feelings in the most natural way possible.

The connection and trust between the photographer and the couple is everything. It's the perfect catalyst to ensure a personalized experience and, consequently, incredible photos.

This is how I manage to go completely unnoticed in the most intimate and emotional moments and portray the most natural and spontaneous images.

Don't be a stranger. Let's chat!

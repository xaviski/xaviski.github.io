Hello there, I’m Xavi Palahí

For the non-traditional, the rule breakers, and the ones who plan to throw a party for the ages.

Hi, I’m Xavi! A destination wedding photographer based in Barcelona. I love capturing the most authentic and natural side of people. Finding goosebumps, portraying the most sincere smiles and looking for uncontrollable tears.

But I want to go much further and delve into your love story. My photojournalist roots and an introverted nature allow me to go unnoticed so as not to alter the emotions of the scene and obtain a very faithful photographic collection of your wedding. So that the memory is real.

Pure photojournalism

Documenting the emotions

In order to photograph a wedding in the most natural way possible, it is essential that I and my cameras go unnoticed. That's when you and all the guests can live the wedding and express all the emotions without embarrassment or discomfort.

This photographic technique allows me to offer you reports that accurately reflect everything that characterized your wedding. So that when the years go by and you go through all the photographs you can really remember what your wedding was like.
